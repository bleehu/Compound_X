==============================
RANGED WEAPONS
==============================
    Outside a weapon's longest range, you cannot hit your target.
Also, weapons cannot shoot over 100m without magnifying sights, or over 150m without 2x+ magnification.


==============================
LONG RIFLES and DESIGNATED MARSKMAN RIFLES (DMRs)
==============================
    The kings of rifles, long rifles are the longest ranged and hardest hitting rifles available.							
On all engagements farther than 200m, you must take 2 Actions to adjust							
your scope for windage and elevation between targets of differing distance.							
Minimum 5 Strength. Bolt Action: take 1 action to fire, 1 action to chamber a new round. 							
Reload time 3 Actions.		

- All Long Rifles/DMRs have "Suppression Level - light" (unless otherwise noted).
