/*========================================
 * This document lists the feats available to characters in Compund X
 * Feats may be taken on every even level as described in the Basic
 * Rules document. Passive feats are used freely with no limit
 * (unless otherwise stated) while active feats require the user to
 * spend a number of nanites, specified next to the feat's name.
 *======================================*/
====================
Passive Feats
====================
Akimbo:
Prerequisites: Dex 7, level 6 and Dual Weild feat
Reduce the miss penalty from dual weilding to 10% each. Reduce the reload 
penalty to reloading to 10%. You may fire at more than one target at once for 
an additional 20% to miss penalty.

Aquired: Additional Plating
Prerequisites: Marksman, Gunslinger
Enables the use of Medium Armour by the listed classes.

Aquired: Battle Ready
Prerequisites: Pilot, Adept
Enables the use of Heavy armour by the listed classes.

Aquired: Metal Jacket
Prerequisites: Soldier, Engineer, Adept, Berserker
Enables the use of Full Armour by the listed classes.

Aquired: The Clandestine Option
Prerequisites: Soldier, Specialist, Pilot, Adept, Hunter
Enables the use of Concealed Armour by the listed classes.

Aquired: The Stealth Option
Prerequisites: Soldier, Engineer, Doctor, Priest, Pilot, Adept, Gunslinger, 
Warlock, Monk, Berserker

Aquired: With Friends Like These . . .
Prerequisites: Pilot, Engineer or Piloting 40

Aquired: . . . Who Needs Nano-Technologies?
Prerequisites: Pilot or "Aquired: With Friends Like These . . ." and Piloting 60

Backstab:
Prerequisites: Level 3 and higher Assassins and Monks.
Any damage dealt to an unaware opponent from behind deals Extra-Crit damage.

Blind Fire: 
Prerequisites:
There are so many bullets flying, you''d rather not peak your head out to look 
at your enemy. +65% firing Penalty, fire from a Hunkered position. Remove any 
buffs from Assisstance atachments, Aiming Attachments, and slings. (its 
reccomended to calculate this separate Miss Chance and write it down next to 
your regular Miss Chance)

Bulltrue:
Prerequisites: Must have training in Shotguns
If an enemy moves from being at least 4m from you to being less than 3m from 
you and you have a loaded shotgun drawn, you may fire at them as a free 
action. If the target is bloodied by the attack, they may not make a melee 
attack against you with their next half action.

Combat Expertise [Guns]: 
Prerequisites:
-15% to miss for weapons use. +5% to melee and -5% to skill checks in combat.

Combat Expertise [Melee]: 
Prerequisites:
(Requirement for Expert Melee Training) -15% to miss in melee. +5% to Guns and
-5% to Skill checks in combat.

Combat Expertise [Support]: 
Prerequisites:
+15% to skill checks for class-related tasks. (if your class is medicine, this 
wouldn''t apply to hacking, etc.). +5% chance to miss with Guns or Melee in 
combat.

Concussive Matainence:
Prerequisites: 20 skill in repair or Engineer class. STR 4
For a half action, you may whack something broken to try to fix it. +20% to 
repair check or take a half action to smack something that you''ve failed to 
repair. +20% to repair check. Regardless, you can only use concusive 
matainence on a target once per encounter.

Conceal Weapon: 
Prerequisites:
Make a stealth check to hide your chosen pistol, shotgun, or smg on your 
person. Minimum score of 20. Roll determines DC for enemy to actively search 
and find weapon.

Double Tap: 
Prerequisites:
Increase your crit range by 10%. Whenever your hit falls into crit range, 
expend an additional bullet in order to deal 2x crit damage. Only Useable with 
Semi-Auto or Full auto weapons. Not applicable to SA Revolvers, Bolt Action, 
Pump-Action, etc.

Dual Weild:
Prerequisites: Dex 6
Maybe you''ve got a bigger corpus colocum and your right brain and left brain
are in higher synch than most people; or maybe you''ve seen too many John Woo
holomovies. Either way, you''ve managed to effectively fight using a weapon
in each hand. The weapons must be of the same type, either two pistols, two
smgs, etc. While weilding dual weapons, you may fire both at the same time
at the same target with a +30% to miss with both of them. You may not
target more than one target at a time. Reloading while dual weilding adds
20% to the difficulty. Additionally the sum of the strength requirements 
must be equal to or less than your strength.

Empty the Mag: 
Prerequisites:
It happens. You aren''t quite sure if you can hit your target perhaps, or 
maybe you round that corner and are staring down the barrel of an enemy AR-68.
Whatever the case, you need to dump the mag in their face to guarantee your 
safety. Roll to pass a DC 30% dex check. on success, you may fire off the 
remainder of your clip or magazine (Applies only to semi- and fully-automatic 
weapons, maximum 10 shots). Roll hits for each shot. Using this feat takes 
3 half actions, and must be completed in the span of one turn. After using 
this feat, the user must reload their weapon for it to be effective again. 
Can only be used once per encounter, LVL number of times per day. At level 5 
and beyond, can be used twice per encounter.

Enhanced Neural Interface: 
Prerequisites:
As a free action, you can use any keyboard, terminal or monitor to perform a 
basic computer inquiry or operation. +10% to ONE pertinent class skill 
(hacking, etc.) while using this feat, to be chosen at the time of taking 
this feat.

First Shot Counts: 
Prerequisites:
When reloading, if you Critically succeed, the first round out of that 
magazine deals Extra-Crit damage.

Focus Energy: 
Prerequisites:
Spend one Full-Action to increase the effects of a spell or Nanite based 
action by 20%, (20% better roll, 20 damage, up to you.)

Four on the Floor:
Prerequisites: Pilot or 20 skill in piloting
Tires were meant to be balded. You get a +20 to any difficult maneuver with a 
land vehicle in which you do not catch air. This works with vehicles that have 
treads or some other method of land support as well.

Gunsmith: 
Prerequisites:
Combine up to 2 weapon total conversions. Ask GM about applicability of 
desired conversations.

Hands of an artist:
Prerequisites: Dex 7 or higher
You use your DEX modifier instead of your PER modifier for rolls to hit with 
ranged weapons. In taking this feat you sacrifice the ability to use PER on 
rolls dealing with using a weapon.

Improvise Weapon:
Prerequisites: Unarmed > 20
You do not take a penalty when using an improvised weapon. Chair legs can be 
used as bats, fan blades as sowrds, etc., etc.

Last Shot Counts: 
Prerequisites:
The last shot in every mag deals Extra-Crit damage, but your reload DC is 
twice as high.

Leaf on the wind:
Prerequisite: Pilot or 40 skill in piloting
You get a +20% to pilot skill checks in airborne or spaceborne vehicles, and
an additional +20% o unconventional maneuvers in such as well, but you and
your vehicle take double damage for the next three turns after using these
bonuses. (any teammates in such a vehicle do not take double damage)

Kata: 
Prerequisites: Level 10 or higher
Whenever you land a successful melee hit against someone, you may roll for X 
additional attacks, where X is your LVL/2. Each additional attack has 20% 
lower chance to hit than the previous hit. (A 4th level character making their 
3rd strike in a row would be rolling at +40% miss chance). This -20% debuff 
applies to a monk's Shadow Strike as well, increasing the DC by 20 per strike 
instead of increasing the strike's miss chance.

Monk:
Prerequisites: Level 7 or higher
Able to use full damage for lethal strikes with unarmed.

Nanite Concentration:
Prerequisites: Level 5
Allows the user to better concentrate on their expenditure of nanites, 
allowing them to achieve one of the following:
    +1 turn of cloaking
    +20% to an combat-action at a cost of 20 nanites for 1 turn (ask GM before applying to an action. )
    +20% to a non-combat-action at a cost of 20 nanites for 1 turn (ask GM before aplying to an action.)
    Others, run any ideas by GM.

Point-man: 
Prerequisites:
When clearing a room, add 10 to your reflex roll.

Pre-emptive Strike: 
Prerequisites:
Whenever you are facing someone and they enter into adjacency with you, you 
gain a pre-emptive melee strike against them.

Quickdraw: 
Prerequisites:
As a free action, draw your chosen weapon into firing position. If you are 
holding something, you can drop it and still draw your weapon as a free 
action. However, you might not want to drop anything glass. Or any explosives.

Rainman: 
Prerequisites: <3 CHA and >8 INT
Your character has something going for them. You may ask the GM how many 
bullets each opponent has left in their weapon.

Run 'N' Gun: 
Prerequisites: PER > 6, DEX > 6 or Soldier, Specialist classes.
you may move at half speed while firing. +10% Miss Chance. If this feat is 
performed while sprinting, half your movment speed before you add the 2m from 
sprinting. The Miss Chance penalties stack. 

Salty Hashtables
Prerequisites: Must be a mechanical race
Your data inputs are sanitized and encrypted, complete with checksums!
Hacking you is 20% harder and you are aware whenever one of your senses is 
malfunctioning.

Salty Space Dog
Prerequisites:
You''ve been up in the black more times than you can count. You get a +10% to
maintaining your bearings during a hyperspace jump as well as a +10% to stellar
navigation.

Shadow Sense:
Prerequisites: Level 12 or higher Monk with Shadow Walk or Shadow Strike.
+10% passive armour bonus with Shadow Strike or +20% passive armour bonus with
Shadow Walk.

Signature Move: 
Prerequisites:
What can you say. When you do what you''re good at, you do it well. Come up 
with a Name or Motto for your signature move, and you gain +10% towards this 
action. HOWEVER, you must shout your Motto or the Name when you perform this 
move. And hey, keep this move on the DL unless it is absolutely necessary to 
be used. You wouldn''t want posers to copy it. (Note: this is an RP''d action 
meant to be performed in cqc, not simply aiming your rifle at their head 
and squeezing the trigger while shouting "BOOM HEADSHOT!" Please ask for 
GM permission when using this feat.)

Sixth Sense:  
Prerequisites:
You have un uncanny knack for reading situations and making accurate decisions 
based off of gut feelings. Gain a skill, Uncanny Insight at 10, and apply it 
to situations where you feel like a bit of extra insight might do you or your 
team a world of good. This skill is maxed out at skill level 20, and only 
upgraded by making good decisions based on this skill, rather than by 
upgrading it with skill points.

Sleight of hand: 
Prerequisites:
Negate the pump of a shotgun, cocking of a revolver, or cycling of a bolt. 
Reload DCs are 5% lower.

Snap Shot: 
Prerequisites:
Time slows down slightly around you, or at least your perception is increased 
allowing you to process everything around you more quickly for a split second. 
When a target is within half your effective range, your hit DC is decreased 
by 10%.

Social Ninja: 
Prerequisites:
Unless sneaking past / hiding from an alert enemy, use a Bluff check in place 
of a Stealth check.

Steady Shot: 
Prerequisites:
Take a full action to breath slowly and line up your shot. -5% miss chance and 
+10m range each per level taken. Maximum 4 levels.

Strong Back: 
Prerequisites: STR 7
You''ve done a lot of lifting and hauling in your time, allowing you to carry 
more than some. Instead of a secondary weapon, you can carry another 
primary weapon.

Suppression: 
Prerequisites:
The first time this feat is taken, enemies shot at must pass a Shock DC of 30 
in order to move or return fire on their turn. Each additional time this feat 
is taken, you may choose to add one of the following onto your suppression 
(each may be taken only once)
    - Add 20% to the Shock save
    - Opponent Attack DC +20% for 1 turn
    - Opponent movement reduced by 5m for 1 turn
    - -20% on a hit against the enemy on your next shot if they move after 
	suppression.

Tele-Cast
Prerequisites: Paladin, Sorcerer, Mage.
You may use a mellee weapon to perform touch effect spells.

Toughness: 
Prerequisites:
Roll a number of dice equal to your FORT / 5 (round up) + 1 and take the 
result in extra hit points, or take the average of the result (1d10 = 5, 
2d10 = 10, can be taken twice).

Upgrade: 
Prerequisites:
Roll a number of dice equal to your INT / 5 (round up) + 1 and take the result 
in extra Nanites, or take the average of the result (1d10 = 5, 2d10 = 10, can 
be taken twice).

Unconventinal Warfare:
Prerequisites: Any feat > 9, combat style must primarily use this stat.
You are trained in the art of combative tossing, or some other obscure 
fighting technique

Unlocked: Boomstick
Prerequisites: Adept, Gunslinger
Enables the use of shotguns by the listed classes.

Unlocked: Weapons Training
Prerequisites: Monk
Enables the use of mellee weapons by the Monk class.

Unlocked: Spray and Pray
Prerequisites: Engineer
Enables the use of SMGs by the Engineer class.

Unlocked: Small Arms Shipment
Prerequisites: Marksman, Doctor, Priest
Enables the use of Carbines and Battle Rifles by the listed classes.

Unlocked: Welcome to the Front Line
Prerequisites: Adept, Paladin
Enables the use of Automatic Rifles by the listed classes.

Very Normal: 
Prerequisites:
Any time that someone would be suspicious of your actions, they will be less 
likely to think twice about what you are doing. Increase your Deceive and 
Speech skills by 10.

Ward:
Prerequisites: Dex 10 or Level 8 or higher assassin / berserker. +20% to 
armour when your melee weapon is equipped.

Wealthy Sponsor: 
Prerequisites:
Gain LUK * Lvl * 750 credits.

Weapon Familiarization (can be taken once): 
Prerequisites:
Remove 15% miss chance from any one weapon model.

Weapon Skill Tree: 
Prerequisites:
(Listed numbers are stacked per level you take in this feat. Maximum 3 levels per tree.)
    - Pistoleer: +10 damage to pistols.
    - Shotgun Surgeon: target gets -10% armour. If they do not have armour deal +5 damage.
    - Spray and Pray: When using SMGs, plus 1 round fired, +10% miss chance.
    - Assault and Battery: +10m range to assault rifles and carbines.
    - Sniper: +5 damage and -5% Miss Chance to Long Rifles
    - HeavyMetal fan: +5% use Heavy Weapons
    - Demo Man: +5 damage explosive radius, +5 skill to use.
    - Penetrating Shot: +10 Bow damage to armoured targets.

====================
Active Feats
====================

Adrenaline Rush [20* X]: 
Prerequisites:
Run double your movement distance for X # ofturns.

Aimed Shot [10 Nanites]: 
Prerequisites:
Add an action to your normal Shooting time. During this time you concentrate 
on your target in order to deal crit damage on your next shot. This is akin to 
seeing a chink in armour and aiming for it, or shooting a target''s arm so 
they cannot use their weapon, etc.

Bunker:
Prerequisites: High-Technology class (Monk, Paladin, Adept, Priest) or 
Knowledge [Nanites] > 40
Expend 25 Nanites, gain 25% to a piece of cover for 4 turns

Flash Dance: 
Prerequisites:
Expend 10*lvl Nanites. Allows the user to make Lvl number of attacks with 
their melee weapon within a 5m space, rolling for each strike. All bonuses 
that would normally apply to a standard attack still apply.

Gun Kata [30 Nanites / Turn]: 
Prerequisites: Level 10 or higher
For up to 3rds, reduce weapon range to 15m, reduce Miss Chance by 50%. Treat 
any weapon as if it were a CQB weapon. After a successful strike, you may roll 
for an additional strike during the same action on any target within the 15m 
range.

Multi-Target [ X*40 Nanites]: 
Prerequisites:
Take 2 actions to concentrate on up to Level +1 number of targets, where X is 
the number of targets. Or wait 1 action to target Level/2(round up) Targets. 
You may Roll once to determine if you hit all of these targets. If you fail 
the roll, then you may roll again to hit your first target normally.

Twitch	[10 Nanites]:
Prerequisites: Level 3, Trained in SMGs or Pistols, Dex > 6
You may aim at an enemy as a free action instead of as a half action. Only 
works with CQB weapons. +15% to miss with the aimed weapon.

Blur	[15 Nanites]:
Prerequisites: Level 10, Twitch
You may aim at an enemy as a free action instead of as a half action. Only 
works with CQB weapons. No Miss Chance penalty.
